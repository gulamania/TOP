Minced Beef with Celery

Description

This recipe is one of my favourites. I got it from a

video
on the youtube channel Chinese Cooking Demystified. Despite the kinda weird name I promise you that the food is actually good. This recipe makes approximately 4 servings and reheats nicely in the microwave if you place a wet rag or paper towel over the bowl.

Ingredients

Steps

Prep

    Celery: remove celery outer layer then to 1/2 cm dice.mix with 1/4 tsp salt in bowl and purge for 10 Aromatics: mince 3-4 cloves of garlic and half inch ginger Meat: marinate beef in 1/4 tsp soy sauce, 1 tsp rice wine, 1/8 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp cornstarch Stir Fry Sauce: mix sauce with 1/2 tbsp soy sauce, 1/4 tsp msg, 1tsp cornstarch, 2 tbsp water.
