Minced Beef with Celery

Description

This recipe is one of my favourites. I got it from a video on the youtube channel Chinese Cooking Demystified. Despite the kinda weird channel name I promise you that the food is actually good. This recipe makes approximately 4 servings when having it over rice and reheats nicely in the microwave if you place a wet rag or paper towel over the bowl.

Ingredients

  • 4 stalks of celery
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 inch of ginger
  • 100-150g of ground of minced beef
  • Bread bean paste aka DouBianJang
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Wine
  • Cornstarch
  • MSG

Steps

Prep

  1. Celery: remove celery outer layer then to 1/2 cm dice.mix with 1/4 tsp salt in bowl and purge for 10
  2. Aromatics: mince 3-4 cloves of garlic and half inch ginger
  3. Meat: marinate beef in 1/4 tsp soy sauce, 1 tsp rice wine, 1/8 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp cornstarch
  4. Stir Fry Sauce: mix sauce with 1/2 tbsp soy sauce, 1/4 tsp msg, 1tsp cornstarch, 2 tbsp water.

Cooking

  1. Put the beef in the pan on medium high with about 2 tbsp of oil until color change (for frozen cook for longer and break up more). If ground rather than minced you want to overcook here
  2. add 1tbsp of broad bean paste and oil, turn heat to low and fry for 90 sec pause mixing
  3. add aromatics and fry for 90 sec
  4. add celery while resume mixing and raise heat to high
  5. add stir fry sauce and another mix then complete
